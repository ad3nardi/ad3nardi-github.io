Stealth Adventure Prototype: This prototype for a steal adventure game saw the development of a player controller and simple enemy AI that would patrol areas designated between nodes and hunt down the player when spotted, investigate the nearby area then engage the player with ranged weapons or return to their previous patrol route. The aim for the player was to make it inside the double story building (large top rectangular area) steal an item and make it to either yellow exit to complete the level. Ensuring challenge through timing guard patrols allowed keen eyed players the ability to time their movement to retain the stealth and take the item, whilst being able to fight back in melee to defeat enemies if that was the player’s desired approach, thus being able to cater for both playstyles was the challenge of the design.
Each ship can be selected an moved, with each healthbar and label representing a different sub-system of the ship which can be damaged to affect that unit’s corresponding capabilities. I.e; damaing weapons reduces damage output.
