Profile

After completing high school Alexander began part time work as a media designer for Systematiq, a consulting firm in Melbourne working with both the marketing and eLearning teams. Using a range of software including the Adobe and Microsoft office suites, Alexander has shown has proven to be adaptable and prompt in acquiring new technical skills, primarily focusing on creating video-based and 3D content. Alexander also is studying design and production at the Academy of Interactive Entertainment (AIE) and is passionate about understanding all aspects from scripting, level design and developing shaders in game engines like Unity to building meshes, rigging and animating in Blender.
