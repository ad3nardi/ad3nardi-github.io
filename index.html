This is my game design portfolio!

2021, I started exploring career opportunities and took my interest to delve into video game design and production where I currently study at AIE (Academy of Interactive Entertainment) and am passionate about understanding all aspects from scripting, level design and developing shaders in game engines like Unity to building meshes, rigging and animating in Blender and Maya.

Alongside study, I also work part-time as a media designer for Systematiq, a consulting firm in Melbourne working with both the marketing and eLearning teams using a range of software including the Adobe and Microsoft office suites, primarily focusing on creating video based content.
